NY Strip with Roasted Mushrooms & Creamed Spinach

Prep: 15m
Cook: 25m
Serves 2

Ingredients

The Meat
  • • 2 (12oz) Sear & Serve NY Strip Steaks
  • • 2 tbsp Unsalted Butter
  • • 2 sprigs Fresh Rosemary
  • • Kosher Salt & Black Pepper
Creamed Spinach
  • • 10oz Fresh Spinach (or frozen, thawed)
  • • 1 small Shallot, minced
  • • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • • 1/2 cup Heavy Cream
  • • 1/4 cup Parmesan Cheese
  • • Pinch of Nutmeg
Mushrooms
  • • 8oz Cremini Mushrooms, sliced
  • • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • • 1 tsp Fresh Thyme

Instructions

1

Prepare the Steak

If product is frozen, thaw overnight under refrigeration. Preheat oven to 400 °F. Open pouch and remove NY Strip Steak. Discard the plastic pouch.
In a pan over medium heat, add 2 tbsp of olive oil. Once hot, add NY Strip Steak and sear the top side for 2 minutes. Flip over and sear the other side for 2 minutes. Remove NY Strip Steak from the pan and transfer to an oven-safe baking sheet. Place baking sheet in the oven and cook for 12-14 minutes.

2

Roast the Mushrooms

Heat a large cast-iron skillet over medium-high heat with olive oil. Add the mushrooms in a single layer (don't crowd the pan, or they will steam instead of brown). Cook undisturbed for 3-4 minutes until golden. Stir, add thyme, salt, and pepper, and cook for another 2 minutes. Remove mushrooms from the pan and set aside.

3

Make the Creamed Spinach

In the same pan, reduce heat to medium. Add a knob of butter, shallots, and garlic. Sauté until fragrant (1 min). Add the spinach in batches, tossing until wilted. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer. Stir in the parmesan and nutmeg. Cook until the sauce thickens and coats the spinach leaves glossy green. Season to taste and keep warm.

4

Serve

Let the steaks rest for 1 minutes before slicing against the grain. Plate the sliced steak alongside a generous helping of the creamed spinach and top with the roasted mushrooms.
