The method

Crafted by Chefs and Sous Vide Precision.

Sear & Serve utilizes sous vide, a French cooking technique. This vacuum and water bath method locks in flavor, maximizes tenderness, and ensures a perfectly even cook. All you have to do is sear and serve.
